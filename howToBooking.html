How To Buy Ticket


STEP 1


Buy tickets

Book your tickets via our online booking page and pay by credit card / debit card or online banking

STEP 2


Booking Confirmation

At the end of your booking process you will receive confirmation email of your booking. The email have details regarding the payment, the order number, product details, customer details and transaction reference

STEP 3


Print your ticket(s)

You can redeem your ticket by presenting order confirmation email at our ticketing counter

NOTES


If you have any issue(s)

If you did not receive the Order Confirmation Email once you has made the payment, please check your junk mail folder in case the email has been marked as spam. In case you still did not received, please contact us at 012-3456789
