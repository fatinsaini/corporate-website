About Us



It takes a lot of good people to make sure the show goes on at Art in Paradise. We’re committed to creating the best experiences possible, and so is every person on our team. From our internationally renowned artists, down to our committed operations and guest services staff, we’re invested in making you happy.


Located within the vicinity of Oriental Village, with 21,000 square feet of build up, it features more than one hundred 3D interactive paintings, artistically painted by a group of talented international artists. This is yet another signature product by Langkawi SkyCab in collaboration with Art In Paradise Langkawi providing new dimension and experience to visitors. Step inside or crawl into the exhibits. Interact with the characters and become the subject of the artwork on display. This almost surreal gallery is a must have on your to-do list. There are nine different theme zones that you will truly enjoy. The different zones include Aquarium Zone, Safari Zone, Fantasy Zone, Optic Illusion Zone, Classic Zone, Main Hall Zone, Interactive Zone 1, Interactive Zone 2 and Glow In The Dark Zone. We aim to leave our visitors with an amazing memory of their time spent at the largest 3D Interactive Art Museum in Malaysia.
